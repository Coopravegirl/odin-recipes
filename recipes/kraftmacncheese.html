Kraft Mac n Cheese

Description

I used to love Kraft Mac and Cheese but now I can't stand it I am putting it here because it has a simple recipe

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Noodles
  • Cheese (powder/slice)
  • Butter
  • Ketchup if you're into that

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add noodles
  3. Cook noodles for like 7 or 8 minutes I think
  4. Stare at the pot longingly
  5. Strain noodles
  6. Add a slice of the individually wrapped American cheese, and also butter and milk and the processed powder stuff
